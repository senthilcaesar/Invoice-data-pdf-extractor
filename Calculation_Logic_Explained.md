# Understanding Your Business Analytics

This document explains the logic used to calculate your business performance. Our goal is to provide a clear picture of your cash flow: what you spend, what intermediaries take, and what you actually keep.

*   **Calculation:** Total Customer Payment - Expenses - Platform Fees.
*   **Simple Meaning:** *"This is the actual amount of money that stays in my pocket after everyone else has been paid."*

---

## 2. The Golden Rule

Every single invoice follows this simple balance:

> **Total Revenue (What the Customer Paid)** 
> **=** 
> **Expenses** + **Platform Fees** + **Net Profit**

## 4. Why Use This Logic?

*   **Honest Profit:** By including Tax in the "Fees" bucket, you ensure your profit isn't "inflated" by money that actually belongs to the government.
*   **Cash Flow Clarity:** It separates "Physical Costs" (what you control) from "Platform Costs" (what the marketplace controls).
*   **Standardized:** This follows e-commerce best practices to ensure you are never surprised by "hidden" costs.
